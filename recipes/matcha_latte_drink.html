Matcha Latte Drink

Description

Highly nutritious and tasty drink

Restaurant grade!

Ingredients

  • Mathca powder
  • Milk
  • Kiwi
  • Green, red or black grapes

Steps

  1. Blend fruit mix of kiwi and grapes
  2. add milk to this mix and blend some more
  3. Boil water to around 60 degrees celcius.
  4. Add some mathca to the water and let it sit for couple of minutes.
  5. Add matcha to original fruit and milk mix
  6. Add some dry ice
  7. Enjoy with your favourite topping!
