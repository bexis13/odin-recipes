Garlic Chicken

Description

Juice filled chicken with a rich garlic sauce flavor! This can easily be done in 15 minutes or less

Pan fried Chicken Breasts in absolutely irresistible garlic filled sauce that soaks and blends well with the greek yougurt! Definitely a comfort food!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chicken spice
  • Greek yogurt
  • Mayonaise
  • Parsley
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. Cut chicken down to desired size
  2. Marinade chicken with spice mix of salt, black pepper, garlic powder and chicken spice
  3. Cut both parsley and Green onion to tiny slices. Mix them together.
  4. Add to vegie mix a sizeable amount of greek yougurt and Mayonaise to your liking. Mix well.
  5. Add to this mix spice mix of salt, black pepper, galic powder, smashed garlic and chicken spice
  6. Marinade your chicken with this mix of vegies, and greek yougurt and Mayonaise.
  7. Fry in a pan with oil for 12 mins. Turn at six minutes mark
  8. Put on plate and spinkle slices of parsley
  9. Enjoy chicken with favourite sauce (like cheese sauce)!
