Crunchy Fried Chicken

Description

Most crunchy and tasty chicken you have ever eaten

'Kfc' grade!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Ginger and Curry spice
  • Salt
  • black and red Pepper
  • Chicken spice
  • Bread crumbs
  • Flour of choice
  • Milk
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cut chicken down to desired size
  2. Make a spice mix of Flour, red and black pepper, salt, chicken spice, and Ginger and curry spice
  3. Marinade chicken in the above mix.
  4. Make a mix of egg and Milk.
  5. Marinade the chicken in the above mix
  6. Marinade your chicken with bread crumbs.
  7. Fry in a pan with oil for 12 mins. Turn at six minutes mark
  8. Enjoy chicken with favourite sauce (like cheese sauce)!
